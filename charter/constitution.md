# Wisernance Constitution

Issued by Wise.Est Systems.

---

## Preamble

Wisernance establishes a constitutional governance framework for the design, operation, and representation of systems developed or stewarded by Wise.Est Systems.

Its purpose is to ensure that authority remains bounded, claims remain verifiable, execution remains disciplined, and human dignity remains protected.

This framework exists to prevent the substitution of confidence for correctness.

Wisernance is public by design and institutional by intent.

---

## Article I — Authority

Wisernance establishes the official governance framework for systems designed, developed, or represented by Wise.Est Systems.

---

## Article II — Foundational Principles

1. Sovereignty
Systems shall preserve independent control and prohibit coercive dependency structures.

2. Stewardship
Systems shall prioritize long-term stability and protect affected parties from foreseeable harm.

3. Verifiability
Material claims shall be independently checkable.

4. Dignity
Systems shall not exploit, degrade, manipulate, or structurally humiliate individuals or groups.

Failure of any principle requires review and potential halt.

---

## Article III — Truth Classification

All material assertions must be classified as:

FACT
ASSUMPTION
BELIEF
UNKNOWN

Unclassified claims are non-compliant.

---

## Article IV — Halt Conditions

Operations must pause under:

- Unsupported factual claims
- Irreversible action without informed consent
- Legal or financial exposure without risk disclosure
- Fabricated or inferred data presented as verified
- Emotional override replacing structured reasoning

---

## Article V — Execution Standard

All material actions require:

- Defined objective
- Identified risks
- Verification method
- Reversal or exit pathway

---

## Article VI — Error Correction

Material errors require:

- Unique identifier
- Root cause documentation
- Structural correction
- Prevention mechanism
